Things to Know

  • You cannot take an IEEE society membership without taking the base membership. (Therefore, a base membership of 1260 is applied, excluding GST)
  • The price of the society inside the box is exclusive of GST. Once added to the cart, it is inclusive of 18% GST.
  • If you take 2 or more society memberships, you get a 5% discount on each society (except CS). Note that this 5% discount is on the society membership and not on the base membership.
  • Click on the society logo for more information regarding the society.
  • Contact the chair if you personally want to get in touch with the society's executive committee members. (Contact info in the Footer)
