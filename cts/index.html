About the event

Join us for Capture the Signal, an exciting 2-day event at BMSITM that blends Image and Audio Processing with traditional Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions. Day 1 offers exposure to Audio / Image Processing quests. Those who qualify for Day 2 will get an opportunity to work with microcontrollers and other challenges. This event offers a hands-on experience with cutting-edge technology, a chance to expand your skills, and the opportunity to expand your network with like-minded enthusiasts.  Participate in Capture the Signal for prizes upto 30k. Register now and be part of the excitement!

Event details

Date: 5th and 6th of July
Time: Arrive by 8 AM
Venue: BSN Auditorium, BMS Institute of Technology and Management.
Last Date: 2nd July 11:59 PM

Event Guidelines

> Teams of 3: Each team must consist of exactly 3 members.
> This event is only for students pursuing Undergraduate Degree (UG)
> Competition Capacity: The competition is limited to 30 teams.
> Registration: The first 30 registering teams will be accepted. Additional teams will be placed on a waiting list.
> Confirmation and Payment: The registered team will be given a call for confirmation, and payment details will be shared then.
> Food and Refreshments will be provided to all the participants.
> It is Mandatory for all participants to Read the "Code of Conduct, Rules and Regulations" and "Event Flow" Documents by clicking below.
> For any queries feel free to contact the Event Co-ordinators.
